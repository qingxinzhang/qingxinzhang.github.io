About

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Chemistry at the University at Buffalo. My research focuses on multiscale materials modeling, nonadiabatic molecular dynamics, and data-driven approaches toward energy-relevant materials.

Research Interests

  • Excited-state processes in materials
